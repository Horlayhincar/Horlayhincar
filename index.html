About AIMAAN

Building Character Through Martial Arts

The Ahmadiyya International Martial Art Association of Nigeria (AIMAAN) was established in 1988 under the directive of Hazrat Khalifatul Masih IV (r.a.) and the leadership of Prof. Mashood Adenrele Fashola, the then National Qa'id (later Sadr) of Majlis Khuddamul Ahmadiyya Nigeria (MKAN). AIMAAN was founded to empower Ahmadi Muslims through self-defense training, physical fitness, and community engagement, with a focus on Taekwondo. It began as a grassroots initiative under the guidance of Grandmaster Nasir O. Yusuf (GHOST), a pioneer in Taekwondo in Nigeria. Over the years, AIMAAN has grown into a prominent force in Nigeria's martial arts landscape, producing thousands of practitioners, champions, and instructors. It is the largest Taekwondo association in Nigeria, with a strong presence across the country and internationally.

Our Mission

To promote martial arts excellence, physical fitness, and moral character development among Nigerian youth through structured training programs rooted in Islamic values of peace, discipline, and respect.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading martial arts organization, producing world-class athletes who exemplify discipline, respect, and sportsmanship on national and international platforms while embodying the motto "Love for All, Hatred for None".

Our Commitment

We uphold the principles of integrity, perseverance, humility, and excellence. Every member is trained to be a positive force in their community through martial arts, contributing to a peaceful and harmonious society.

Enhancing the skills of our referees and coaches to maintain high standards in competitive Taekwondo.

Security Seminar

Our Security Seminar series offers comprehensive training sessions designed to empower individuals and communities with practical knowledge for enhancing personal and digital safety.

An exciting championship showcasing young talents under 18 in the Taekwondo community.

National Camp

The National Camp is our premier week-long intensive, uniting members from across the country for a transformative experience of advanced training, leadership development, and deep camaraderie.
